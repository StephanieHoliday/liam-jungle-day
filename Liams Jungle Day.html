🌴 Liam's Jungle Day 🐒

An Interactive English Learning Adventure!

📚 The Story

👋 Hi! My name is Liam and I'm from Ireland. But now I live in the Amazon jungle in Brazil! I study monkeys, birds, and frogs. My day is very different here!

🕓 I get up at 4 o'clock in the afternoon. I have a quick shower, then I eat breakfast with my friends.

🎒 At 5:30, we pack our bags.

🌳 At 6 o'clock, we walk into the forest.

🦜 We look at birds and monkeys. We don't talk — we want to be very quiet. ✏️ We write the animals' names in our notebooks.

🥪 At 9:30, we sit and eat sandwiches. 🧃 We drink juice or water. It's our lunch!

🐸 At 10:15, we look for frogs and other night animals. 🔦 We use flashlights because it is dark.

⏰ We finish our work at 3 o'clock in the morning.

💻 Before dinner, we write notes on the computer.

🍽️ We eat at 5:15 a.m., and at 5:45, I go to bed. 😴

☀️ I don't see the sun, but I love my jungle day!

📚 Vocabulary Box

get up ⏰ - to leave your bed in the morning or evening
go to bed 😴 - to lie down and sleep
have breakfast/lunch/dinner 🍽️ - to eat food in the morning, afternoon, or evening
look at 👀 - to use your eyes to see something
write ✏️ - to put words or names on paper
eat 🍔 - to take food into your mouth
drink 🧃 - to take liquid into your mouth
flashlight 🔦 - a small light you carry in your hand
quiet 🤫 - not loud, very soft
dark 🌌 - no light or very little light
